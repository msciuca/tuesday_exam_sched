About

Hey! I’m Diana. I teach high school engineering in Vegas. I build fun STEM lessons—think 3D prints, Arduino gadgets, and hands-on challenges.

I have a Neuroscience degree and a teaching license. I mix tech know‑how with creative lessons. My fave projects? Student‑built seesaws, arcade controllers, and solar ovens.

Beyond class, I’m fluent in Fusion 360, Python, and web dev. I snagged two $1,200 grants to upgrade our makerspace fab lab. Now I’m starting a nonprofit to fix up laptops, teach coding skills, and donate to under‑resourced schools.

In my free time, I climb Nevada rocks, whip up vegan meals, and snap pics at Rainbow Gardens. Weekends? Exploring Sloan Canyon or plotting my next trip.

I’m launching “Organic Mechanic” on YouTube—quick health tips and DIY tech. Stay tuned!
